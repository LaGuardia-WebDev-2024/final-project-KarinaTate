Designing a House For this Caterpillar I Found

The insect you see above is Elmer. And although that leaf he's standing on right now is a very practical and adequate living space on its own, Elmer's longs for a new home. A change of scenery if you will.

This is where this website comes in. I've taken it upon myself to find the perfect living quarters for our many-legged friend.
